⚡ WELCOME TO NOVA TECH FEST 1998 ⚡
[ BEYOND THE BROWSER ]

Nova Tech Fest is the premier annual gathering for hackers, builders, and visionaries on the bleeding edge of technology. Now in its 3rd year, NovaFest '98 brings together 500+ attendees across 3 days of workshops, talks, and competitions.

Held at the Silicon Valley Convention Center, this is where the future gets written — one line of code at a time.
